let's See Some Missions

Mission 1: Apollo 11

The first successful manned mission to land on the Moon.

Mission 2: Mars Rover

The rover explored the Martian surface to gather data about the planet.

Mission 3: Voyager 1

Launched in 1977, it is now the farthest spacecraft from Earth.

Mission 4: Hubble Space Telescope

The telescope captured images of distant galaxies and stars.

Mission 5: SpaceX Falcon 9

The first reusable rocket launched by SpaceX.

Mission 6: International Space Station

A collaborative space station for scientific research orbiting Earth.

Mission 7: New Horizons

Visited Pluto and provided the first close-up images of the dwarf planet.

Mission 8: Curiosity Rover

The rover explored Mars to investigate its climate and geology.

Mission 9: Chandra X-ray Observatory

Provides X-ray images of space phenomena like black holes and supernovae.

Mission 10: Perseverance Rover

The rover landed on Mars to search for signs of past life and collect samples.
